Kaiser A ÖzHun

Lund, Sweden

Accomplished Artist Bridging Traditional and Contemporary Practices

Kaiser A. ÖzHun’s geometric abstractions emerge from a search for clarity, balance and inner resonance. His shapes—arcs, spheres, vessels and ascending forms—function as symbols of interior movement, suggesting transitions, thresholds and quiet states of awareness.

Rather than depicting external subjects, these paintings explore the choreography of perception itself. Color becomes atmosphere: soft gradients, glowing contrasts and measured tones form spaces that feel meditative, weightless and psychologically open.

Together, the works form a visual path through stillness and intensity, mapping how geometry can hold emotion, memory and spiritual presence in a contemporary abstract language.

Exhibition Introduction (English)

Kaiser A. ÖzHun — Geometry of the Soul presents a body of work where classical art training expands into a contemporary geometric language. Educated through rigorous studio programs in China and Europe— including Renaissance-based MFA studies and a Master’s in East Asian Art— ÖzHun enters abstraction with rare discipline and refinement.

These paintings mark a shift from figuration and historical research toward a modern, intuitive visual form. Pigmented surfaces, subtle textures and balanced color fields create compositions that feel both architectural and emotional. Curves, lines and chromatic spaces move with the rhythm of thought, forming quiet, meditative environments.

Through this evolving abstract vocabulary, ÖzHun redefines his artistic voice and invites viewers into the unfolding geometry of the human spirit.

Sergi Giriş Metni (Türkçe)

Kaiser A. ÖzHun — Ruhun Geometrisi, klasik sanat eğitiminin çağdaş geometrik soyuta dönüşümünü sunar. Çin ve Avrupa’daki akademik eğitim, Rönesans temelli MFA ve Doğu Asya Sanatı yüksek lisansı, sanatçıya disiplinli ve derinlikli bir yaklaşım kazandırmıştır.

Bu resimler, figürasyon ve tarihsel araştırmadan sezgisel ve modern bir dile geçişin göstergesidir. Zengin pigmentler, ince dokular ve dengeli renk alanları hem mimari hem duygusal bir atmosfer oluşturur. Eğriler, çizgiler ve renk kütleleri düşüncenin ritmiyle hareket ederek dingin, meditasyon benzeri alanlar yaratır.

ÖzHun, bu gelişen soyut dil ile sanatçı kimliğini yeniden tanımlar ve izleyiciyi insan ruhunun geometrisine davet eder.
