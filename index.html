Kaiser A ÖzHun

Lund Sweden

Accomplished Artist Bridging Traditional and Contemporary Practices

I am a multidisciplinary artist with a background in both visual arts and art history. My journey began with classical training in China and continued with an MFA grounded in Renaissance techniques, followed by a Master’s in East Asian Art from Sotheby’s Institute of Art.

My work blends traditional methods—such as ink, drawing, and pigment—with contemporary forms and digital media, including AI-assisted tools. I’m particularly inspired by the Buddhist cave art of the Tarim Basin (Kizil, Khotan, Turfan) and the cultural exchanges across Central Asia, China, Korea, and Japan. Through this lens, I explore identity, history, and the spiritual in visual culture.
